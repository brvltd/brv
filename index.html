About Us



BRV_logistics Limited is a tailored logistics services that offers overland transportation, air freight, sea/ocean freight, packaging and large projects services. It is an integrated end to end shipping company, air freight, Sea freight and clearing & forwarding agent company based in Nairobi Kenya. We have differentiated ourselves in the market place by adopting a culture of focused, impeccable and dedicated customer service.

We have steadily grown and provide the full range of logistics services to our customers by expanding our customer base beyond the East African region to a global platform.

BRV_logistics Limited is one of the leading providers of freight forwarding and supply chain management services. We afford our customers the very best in logistical solutions by sourcing for the most competitive rates and service providers, coupled with our own operational technology. We have been providing our clients with logistics solutions that support their trade positively affecting the way they do business, wherever they are found in the globe.
